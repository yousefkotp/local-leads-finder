Frequently Asked Questions

What is Local Leads Finder?

Local Leads Finder is a web application for agencies and marketers who need verified local business leads enriched with contact details, Google Maps profiles, and export-ready datasets.

How does Local Leads Finder collect business information?

The tool connects to Google Maps through the Decodo API to gather public business listings, then enriches them with phone numbers, websites, emails, ratings, and review counts.

Can I export my leads?

Yes. Once a search completes you can download your leads as CSV or JSON files, ready for CRM imports, email outreach, or reporting.

Do I need technical skills to use the tool?

No coding is required. Pick a location, choose a business type, and Local Leads Finder automates the rest with guided onboarding and an interactive map.
